% Expected-hard regression: if Mike's price M is twice John's positive price J,
% then Mike's price is greater.  Negating this universal conjecture creates
% Skolem constants, not variables.  Numeric variable instantiation alone
% therefore cannot make the arithmetic ground; genuine theory reasoning or
% theory-aware abstraction would be needed.
fof(cars_general,conjecture,
  (! [J,M] :
    (((($is_number(J) & $is_number(M))
       & ($product(2,J) = M))
       & $greater(J,0))
     => $greater(M,J)))).
