% Regression: find a positive price where Mike's car costs twice as much as
% John's, and report whose is more expensive.  J = 1 and M = 2 make all
% three arithmetic constraints false and leave the answer $ans(mike).
%
% The positivity condition matters: "twice as expensive" alone does not
% imply a strict comparison for zero or negative mathematical values.
$product(2,J) != M | -$greater(J,0) | -$greater(M,J) | $ans(mike).
